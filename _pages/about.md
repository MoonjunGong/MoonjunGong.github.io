I have been a visitor in [AI4CE LAB] at [New York University(NYU)](https://www.nyu.edu/), working with [Yiming Li](https://roboticsyimingli.github.io/), advised by [Prof. Chen Feng](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=YeG8ZM0AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao) since Mar.2023.
